What Uplink Does
Everything you need to level up
Three powerful tools in one seamless platform for everyone — students, creators, entrepreneurs, and businesses.
🎯
Goal Tracker
Set goals, break them into steps, and get cheered on by your network. Progress is visible on your profile so the right people find you.
🧠
Skill Sharing
Teach what you know, learn what you don't. Short lessons and tutorials posted by real members — not generic YouTube videos.
💼
Opportunity Board
Businesses post gigs, jobs, and collabs. Apply directly with your profile. Smart matching surfaces the right opportunities automatically.

Smart Networking

Direct Messaging
Chat directly with connections, collaborators, and clients. Clean, fast, distraction-free — no algorithm deciding who sees your messages.
💳
Payments & Billing
Upgrade your plan with secure simulated payments. Full billing history, saved payment methods, and instant receipts.
